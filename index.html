Top three activities to do in Riga

Old Riga

The Old Town Riga is the oldest part of our capital city, it is a city center, as well as a favorite place for recreation and entertainment

Splendid Palace

Cinema "Splendid Palace" features auteur, art house films, festival favourites from Europe and other world regions.

Riga Canal Boat

Take a cruise along the Daugava River and Riga City Canal and see the most popular sights of the historic city center.
